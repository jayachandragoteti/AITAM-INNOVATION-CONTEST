    About Us

    Are you locked in the boredom of COVID-19!!! Wash your brains and get ready to enter the space of innovation and be a part of AITAM's Innovation Challenge This is a State Level Competition being conducted by Aditya Institute of Technology and Management , Tekkali in association with MHRD-IIC cell, to utilize the period of lockdown for productive development. This contest is a way to receive breakthrough ideas and solutions which addresses innovation challenges in this amid lockdown. AITAM's Innovation Challenge mainly focuses on WHAT are the problems facing by large, medium and small companies as well as non-profit organisation in this pandemic scenario. This can be used to invoke the creative ideas and also one’s own talent who have got the chance to prove themselves by to this challenge. We feel that there is no age limit for an individual for implementing the Innovative Ideas and thus how become an entrepreneur.

    This is a 2-stage contest in which there will be refining process at each stage. Students should select any problem statement of their choice from the thrust areas mentioned and can propose their solution.

    GUIDELINES
    Rules for stage-1

    Poster/Abstract Presentation(category-1, category-2)

    • Poster/Abstract should contain the problem statement & personal details such as name of Participant , school name, class.
    • Poster/Abstract should be submitted in PDF format.
    • Poster/Abstract must contain
    1. Introduction
    2. Material Required
    3. Scientific Principle Involved
    4. Construction And Working Of Exhibit/Model
    5. Social Unitilty

    (For any kind of conversions from a file format to PDF. Use the link https://smallpdf.com/pdf-converter)
    • The poster should have HD quality when sent in the PDF format. (This depends on the way you scan your poster.)
    • Poster Guidelines

    • Layout and format must be organized and concise
    • Use brief and simple language, better to avoid text altogether
    • Present your message in a clear and logical way
    • Have a unique feature to attract the audience
    • Choose a catchy but conceptual title
    • Get rid of unnecessary details
    • Check everything before printing/Drawing.


    Abstract Presentation(category-2,categoty-3)

    • Abstract should contain the problem statement & personal details such as name, Institute name, Year of Study.
    • Abstract should be submitted in PDF format. For any kind of conversions from a file format to PDF. Use the link https://smallpdf.com/pdf-converter
    • The Abstract Drawings (if any)should have HD quality when sent in the PDF format. This depends on the way you scan your poster.
    • Abstract Guidelines
    1. The solution should be summarized into a paragraph
    2. Describe the solution found
    3. Method used to solve the problem
    4. Results found
    5. Analysis
    6. Conclusion
