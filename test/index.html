About Us

Are you locked in the boredom of COVID-19!!! So, wash your brains and get ready to enter the space of innovation and be a part of AITAM Innovation Contest. This is a State Level Competition conducted by Aditya Institute of Technology and Management to utilize the period of lockdown for productive development. This contest is a way to receive breakthrough ideas and solutions to important innovation challenges in this amid lockdown. AITAM Innovation Contest mainly focuses on facing large, medium and small companies as well as non-profit organisation in this pandemic scenario.
